FAQs

Q. How is this website better than any other recipe site?

This website is not meant to be be better than other sites, it just specializes in certain recipes to help anybody who may need it. If you need to use another site, that's great, we wish you the best in your culinary adventures.

What are some other recipes that are cheap and easy to make?

We can't cover every easy recipe in existence, but there are many recipes using pasta, rice, potatoes, or chicken that are quite easy to make, and we encourage you to explore those areas for some possible new go-to meals.

What are some additional things to add to these recipes?

You could add beans or meat to quesadillas to spice them up a bit. For spaghetti, add italian sausage and parmesan to give off more flavor. And for baked potatoes, there is always sour cream, bacon, green onions, and chives you could add on for an extra kick.
